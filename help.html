Chemical Water Pollution

Overwiew

Water pollution occurs when undesirable foreign substances are introduced into natural water.The substances may be chemical or biologial in nature. Common pollutants human or animal waste, dicease-producing organisms, radioactive materials, toxic metals, agricultural chemicals, acid rain, and high temperature water.

How did this Happen?

For much of history, humans used waterways and bodies of water as waste dumps. When the human population was low, fewer people were exposed to the effects of pollution, and the resourses were fewer and produced less pollution. During the Industrial Revolution of the 19th century, water pollution was recognized as a danger to public health.
